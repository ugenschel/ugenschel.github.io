About me

Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics

I have been an Assistant professor in the Department of Statistics at Iowa State since Fall 2009. My research interests are Reliability, Robust Statistics and Statistics Education. I also serve as the Advisor to the Undergraduate Statistics Club. I enjoy teaching and like to get to know my students - so don’t be surprised if I know you by name. Teaching has always been a passion of mine and I care about every student’s success. I work hard to provide a positive learning experience in the classroom but I expect my students to work hard as well.

  • My e-mail address is ulrike at iastate.edu.
