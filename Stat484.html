STAT 484: Computer Processing of Scientific Data (Dual-listed with STAT 584). (3-0) Cr. 3. F. Prereq: STAT 301 or STAT 326 or STAT 401 or STAT 587 Structure, content and programming aspects of modern statistical software packages. Advanced techniques for data management, graphics, exploratory data analysis, and generalized linear models. Credit for only one of STAT 479, STAT 484, or STAT 584 may be applied to graduation. May not be used for graduate credit in the Statistics MS and PhD degree programs.
